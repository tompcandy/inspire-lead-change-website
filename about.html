About

Operators turned coaches.

Inspire Lead Change exists because we've sat in the chairs our clients sit in — and we know what helps and what doesn't.

What we believe

Excellence is built, not bought.

We don't sell decks. We coach the people who'll still be there after the engagement ends.

People protect what they build

Systems imposed from outside last as long as the consultant's contract. Systems built by your people last as long as you need them to.

Trust is the precondition

Behaviour change without trust is compliance — and compliance evaporates. We build trust first, every time.

Coaching beats consulting

Telling your team what to do builds dependency. Coaching them through it builds capability. We pick capability.

Meet the founder

Who's behind ILC.

TC

Thomas Candy

Founder & Lead Coach

Tom has 12 years' experience leading Operational Excellence programmes across multiple industries and sectors, coaching everyone from frontline supervisors to multinational executives. He served as a senior leader in the Operational Excellence team at PA Consulting and previously worked for PwC in both the UK and Canada.

Before consulting, Tom spent five years at British Sugar managing operations and maintenance across all four UK manufacturing sites — so when ILC talks about understanding what it takes to deliver in the real world, that experience is where it comes from.

He founded Inspire Lead Change to bring an unapologetically coaching-led approach to a market that too often defaults to short-term fixes and external dependency.
